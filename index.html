My Studies

I am a senior at Pitt double majoring in Communication Rhetoric and Media & Professional Communications and minoring in Studio Arts. I am curious, question a lot of things, and love to write and create. I am particularly interested in the rhetoric of advocacy efforts, how social initiatives develop online, and media and rhetorical ethics. My majors and minor have allowed me to connect my interests through research, writing, and artistic endeavors (mostly digital, some physical).

Knowledge, Tools, and Skills

I would consider myself a maker, thinker, and critic above all, and a writer when the moment strikes. I often struggle to find a balance between these sides of myself. I worked doing creative at an advertising agency for 8 months where I got to practice many facets of digital creation and got to know a bunch of Adobe creative software quite well. Much of my work there was graphic design focused, but I also had the opportunity to write scripts, edit audio and video advertisements, and practice some animation. I've been working on a research project for the past year which has given me a chance to practice my writing skills, but I don't have nearly as much fun with that. I hope to further develop my audio and video creation and editing skills in this course, and work with those skills more artistically than practically.

Potential Project

I am incredibly interested in law and policy, media ethics, and how these topics and conversations will continue to evolve in the digital age. I think there are important questions to ask when considering the relationship between people and technology. In a photography class, I made a quick project that questioned the ethics of street photography, and it sparked futher thought for me on what it means to live in a society where we are constantly surveilled, what privacy means in the 21st century, etc. etc. There's a lot to work though there and much more research to be done on my end. I hope this course provides an outlet for me to further consider those questions.

In the Future

Post-graduation I plan to work in advertising doing creative for a few years. I can see myself working towards becoming a creative director, but I don't think this is what I want to do longterm. Ideally I will go back to school, for what I am not sure. I have considered a masters in media communications or going to law school because either of these spaces would allow me to further immerse myself in topics that I find interesting and important. Either are a big commitment though so I need some time to figure all that out.
